NoPause

In today's world it's hard to find a place to make your mark. In one of my branding classes junior year at Parsons, I was asked to work on a project that would help people from Generation X (Gen X - ages 38 to 53). Right away I thought about some of the women in my life around this age. Quite often they all seem to mention menopause as a big stressor in their lives and I wanted to take this opportunity to explore that. This is how NoPause was born. Now, although this project has not gone any further than the brand book you see here, I hope that someday in the future I can continue my work on this subject as a passion project.

There are far too many women going through this completely natural, healthy experience for there to be so few resources for them to use on a daily basis.

So, for the puprose of this project I knew I had to learn all about my target audience (the women of gen X) in order to create something meaningful. Of course, there is a lot more information regarding gen X women than what is shared here, but I narrowed it down to what I felt were the most relevant facts to my brand.

After this extensive research, I started to ideate and create the different parts of NoPause like the color scheme, the technical do's and don'ts, and possible products and services.

In terms of the brand's color scheme I did some research and found that these colors I've chosen for the brand inspire strength, calm, and trust.

With the colors established, I then went into the other parts of the brand.
